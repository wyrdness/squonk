## Overview

The Squonk is the saddest fearsome critter - a hideously ugly creature with warty, saggy skin that weeps constantly over its appearance. When captured or seen, it dissolves completely into a pool of tears.

**Unique Trait**: Only sympathetic fearsome critter - evokes pity rather than fear or laughter.

---

## Characteristics

- **Appearance**: Hideously warty, saggy skin
- **Behavior**: Weeps constantly over own ugliness
- **Habitat**: Pennsylvania hemlock forests
- **Escape Method**: Dissolves into tears when captured
- **Result**: Impossible to keep as proof

---

## Cultural Legacy

**Music**: Genesis - "Squonk" (1976)
**Significance**: Most memorable tragic creature in folklore

## Status: Folklore

Pennsylvania tall tale, notable for evoking sympathy rather than fear.
